## Payment Instructions

### Step-by-Step Payment Process

1. **Book Appointment**
   - Patient books consultation through Dr. Saathi app
   - System generates consultation ID and amount

2. **Make Bank Transfer**
   - Transfer exact amount to Dr. Saathi bank account
   - Use Consultation ID as reference/remarks
   - Keep transaction receipt

3. **Upload Payment Proof**
   - Take photo of bank transfer receipt
   - Upload in Dr. Saathi app under "Payment Verification"
   - Include: Transaction ID, Date, Amount

4. **Payment Verification** (within 2-4 hours)
   - Admin verifies payment
   - Consultation is confirmed
   - Patient receives SMS confirmation

5. **Attend Consultation**
   - Visit doctor at scheduled time
   - After consultation, bill is auto-generated
   - Bill shows payment as "PAID via Bank Transfer"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I pay using mobile wallets like eSewa or Khalti?**
A: Currently, Dr. Saathi accepts only Bank Transfer for consultation fees. Digital wallet integration (eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay) is planned for future updates.

**Q: How long does payment verification take?**
A: Typically 2-4 hours during business hours. Upload clear photo of receipt for faster processing.

**Q: What if I forget to include reference in bank transfer?**
A: Upload the receipt with your Consultation ID written on it, and our team will verify manually.

**Q: Can someone else make payment on my behalf?**
A: Yes, but include your Patient ID and Consultation ID in the remarks. Any name can be used for transfer.

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes, all payments go directly to Dr. Saathi's registered company bank accounts. All transactions are logged and audited.
