## Payment Flow

### Before Consultation
1. Patient books appointment
2. **Patient pays consultation fee** via:
   - Bank Transfer (Only accepted method)
   - Note: Digital wallets (eSewa/Khalti/IME Pay) may be added in future
3. Payment recorded in system
4. Consultation scheduled

### After Consultation
1. Doctor completes consultation
2. Doctor enters diagnosis and prescription
3. Doctor adds any additional charges (if applicable)
4. Doctor clicks "Complete & Generate Bill"
5. **System automatically:**
   - Marks payment as "PAID"
   - Generates PDF bill with logo
   - Sends bill via SMS
   - Sends bill via Email (PDF attachment)
   - Sends push notification
   - Stores bill in patient's account

## Bill Generation Specifications

### File Format
- **Format**: PDF (A4 size)
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI
- **Margins**: 30px all sides
- **Orientation**: Portrait

### Filename Convention
```

```

### Logo Requirements

- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Quality**: High resolution (minimum 512x512 source)
