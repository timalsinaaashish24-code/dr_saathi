Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026

Dr. Saathi ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Dr. Saathi" or the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name, date of birth, age, and gender
  • Contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Address and emergency contact details
  • Account credentials (email, encrypted password)

1.2 Health Information

  • Medical history and current health conditions
  • Allergies and medication information
  • Prescriptions and diagnoses
  • Consultation records and treatment plans
  • Insurance information (company, policy number, member ID)

1.3 Location Data

  • GPS location for finding nearby healthcare facilities
  • Location data for air quality monitoring features

1.4 Device and Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing healthcare services including patient registration, appointment booking, and telemedicine consultations
  • Generating and managing medical prescriptions and billing
  • Sending appointment reminders and health notifications via SMS
  • Facilitating communication between patients, doctors, and pharmacies
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements in Nepal

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using:

  • Local storage: Health records are stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite databases for offline access
  • Cloud storage: Data is synced to secure cloud servers when internet connectivity is available
  • Encryption: All passwords are hashed using SHA-256. Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access controls: Role-based access ensures doctors can only see their patients' data

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal or health information. We may share data with:

  • Healthcare providers: Your doctors and pharmacies you authorize
  • Payment processors: For billing and payment transactions
  • Legal authorities: When required by law or court order

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and health data stored in the app
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your health records
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Medical records are retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by Nepal's healthcare regulations. You may request account deletion at any time, after which non-essential data will be removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Dr. Saathi is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without parental consent.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

  • Firebase (Google) for analytics and crash reporting
  • Map services for location-based features
  • SMS services for appointment reminders

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
